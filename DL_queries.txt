#1 All mammals that are larger than 1.5 meters:

Mammal and hasSize some xsd:decimal[>1.5]


#2 All animals that live in a forest and are smaller than 1.5 meters:

Vertebrate and hasHabitat some Forest and hasSize some xsd:decimal[<1.5]


#3 Vertebrates introduced to United_Kindom

Vertebrate and introducedToCountry value United_Kindom


#4 Mammals that have at least 2 predators

Mammal and hasPredator min 2


#5 All animals that have as predator the eagle and are not birds: 

Animal and hasPredator value Eagle and not(Bird)
